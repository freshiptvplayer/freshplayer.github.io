# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** June 2026

This privacy policy governs your use of the mobile application **Free IPTV Player** (hereafter referred to as "the Application") for mobile devices, which was created by **Free Iptv Player Dev Team** (hereafter referred to as "the Developer").

---

## 1. Information Collection and Use

The Application does not explicitly require users to register or provide personally identifiable information to function. However, the Application utilizes third-party services that may collect information used to identify you.

### 1.1 Third-Party Services & Ad Networks
To provide ad monetization, this Application integrates **Google AdMob**. AdMob uses software development kits (SDKs) to collect and process data to serve personalized and non-personalized advertisements based on your interests.

The types of data collected by AdMob may include:
* IP addresses (which may reveal coarse/approximate location)
* Advertising IDs (such as the Android Advertising ID)
* Device information (model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers)
* App interaction data (ad impressions and clicks)

For more detailed information regarding how Google uses data when you use this Application, please review:
* [Google Partner Privacy & Terms Policy](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites)
* [Google Play Services Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

### 1.2 Server-Side Data Processing
To ensure optimal application performance and stability on budget-friendly devices (such as streaming sticks and TVs), the Application utilizes an external server backend to temporarily process and parse streaming data playlist files, URLs, or authentication credentials provided by the user.

* **No Permanent Storage:** This data is processed strictly in-memory or on a transient basis to parse, format, and deliver the content back to your device. The Developer does not save, store, or log your personal playlist content or credentials on any permanent server database.
* **Data Transmission & Protocol Notice:** While the Application securely communicates with the Developer's backend using encrypted protocols (HTTPS) wherever possible, the Application interacts with third-party content providers. Because many third-party streaming links and playlists still rely on unencrypted HTTP protocols, data transmitted between the backend and those specific provider endpoints may be unencrypted. Users are advised to use trusted networks and providers.

## 2. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Ad serving partners like AdMob may use cookies, web beacons, or unique device identifiers to anonymously track user behavior, target ads, and measure ad performance within the Application.

## 3. How to Opt-Out of Interest-Based Advertising

You can manage or opt-out of personalized advertising directly through your Android device settings:
1. Open your device's **Settings**.
2. Navigate to **Privacy** > **Ads** (or **Google** > **Ads** depending on your OS version).
3. Select **Reset advertising ID** or enable **Opt out of Ads Personalization**.
